Sushi Class: Rolls & Nigiri

@ Meal Cozy

Roll Your Own Delicacy
Join Chef Joshua for a lesson in the basics of making your own rolls and nigiri. Learn to make simple rolls as well as some specialties, including a few of the chef's own creations like the Big Boss Roll and the Fraser Roll.

Learn from the Best
Learn how to handed pick a selection of fish based on what's available at the market. Hands on experience with the Chef to teaches you how to best prepare each fish. Learn to simply cut fish for nigiri and how to set up beautiful plates. Plus, try some classic Japanese appetizers. Select a handy kit for making sushi and pick up the addtional incredients right at the shop.

BYOB
Bring wine, beer, or sake to share with the class and sip to go along with the rolls.

6pm, Friday, March 27

$59 / person
